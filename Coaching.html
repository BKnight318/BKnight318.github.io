OW2 Support Guide

For amatures 

About

Overwatch 5v5 first-person shooter in which players fight to complete an objective. There are three roles which teams build their compositions with. Each role has a unique part to play to give a team its best chance at winning.

This page will be aimed directly towards the support role.
I believe support is the most important role in overwatch,
it is important for these characters to be understood and played correctly in order for a game to go well.

 

(clip of me showing healing output of each hero)

 

Main Healer

This role is mostly used to keep your team alive. The heroes generally have more sustain/burst healing than off-healers, these heroes include-

Ana Baptise Kiriko Illari Moiraon

Off Healer

This role also heals decently, but the main objective is to implement non-healing abilities in order to secure a kill (Examples: damage boost, Speed boost, discord orb). These heroes include-

Lucio Mercy Lifeweaver Zenyatta

 

Mercy

Though mercy is easy to start on, she has a high skill ceiling for those willing to put time into her.  There are two things that make top mercy players differ from average mercy players

Survivability

(clip 1 a clip of me as mercy navigating a team fight talking about good positioning)

A really important aspect of Mercy’s playstyle is uptime. Mercy brings value to the team just by being alive. Every mercy player should value their own life over others, meaning mercy players shouldn’t put themselves in dangerous situations. Use cover as an advantage, every object that blocks off enemy sight line is your friend, DO NOT STAND IN THE OPEN

Beam Priority

(clip2 I would display how powerful damage boost is compared to how strong a hero is without it)

Mercy's main value comes directly from her damage boost. Although she can heal, every mercy player should prioritize damage boosting rather than healing. Good Damage boost targets include heroes that have high burst damage(high damage over a short time): -Ashe, Soldier 76, Phara, Sojourn, echo While damage boosting is a main priority, mercy also should be keeping the other support alive. Always watch out if your other support is getting pressured, and help out as much as you can in order to keep them safe.
